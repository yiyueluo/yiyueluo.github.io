Mission


We take an interdisciplinary approach to push the boundaries of wearable technology and AI integration for impactful applications in healthcare, robotics, and human-computer interactions. Our mission is to bridge the gap between wearable interfaces and artificial intelligence, advancing toward end-to-end integrated intelligent wearable systems to record, model, and augment physical interactions. We particularly focus on the scalability, seamless integration, robustness, and adaptivity of the systems.
