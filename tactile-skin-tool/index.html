Overview

The Tactile Skin Toolkit grew out of research conducted at MIT on scalable tactile sensing. We include a list of materials The goal is for the Tactile Skin Toolkit to be a useful and accessible shared resource that can help advance different fields. We hope to continue to improve and expand the Toolkit.
